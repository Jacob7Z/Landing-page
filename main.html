IBANEZ AZ

Taking adaptability to new extremes, the AZ series has established itself and carved out a place that spans everything from the warmest blues licks, to the most intense, shred-heavy metal riffs. Carrying this ethos forward, the AZ line embodies time-tested Ibanez innovation and player-focused features like a smooth, oval neck grip, a well-balanced asymmetrical body shape, and the neck heel allowing for unrivaled playability.

PRODUCS

Ibanez, a Japanese brand owned by Hoshino Gakkai, is one of the most popular guitar manufacturers worldwide. With collaboration with Frank Zappa and Steve Vai, Ibanez introduced iconic models called the Ibanez JEM and the Ibanez Universe. Ibanez also introduced, in the superstrat segment, the RG series which were a lower priced version of the Ibanez JEM model. Ibanez GIO series has delivered few of the most popular budget guitars. Ibanez GRG series, part of the GIO line, delivers few of the most selling guitars out of which Ibanez GRG170DX and Ibanez GRG270 being the most popular models worldwide. Ibanez produces high quality Acoustic, Electric Guitars, Bass Guitars, Classical Guitars, Ukulele & Accessories.
